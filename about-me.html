wagtail
This is me, a wagtail. At least, that’s the nickname my Scouting friends gave me based upon my character traits. Even though it has been almost 20 years now, I think this nickname still suits me well.

A wagtail is characterized by the constant bouncing of its long, dark tail. It is very lively, always busy and cheerful in nature. It likes company, playing and frolicking and is sometimes restless.
mountain life
I love being in the mountains and the simple lifestyle that comes with it. My favorite activity is hiking from cabin to cabin, on foot or with skis, together with my boyfriend.

I enjoy going back to basics: moving on my own, carrying everything I'll need in my backpack, making a fire for cooking, sleeping outdoors or in remote mountain cabins, living to the rhythm of nature.

My dream is to one day move to the Aosta valley, where I took the picture on the homepage.
outdoor sports
In my spare time I like to do outdoor sports, such as trail running, hiking, surfing, rock climbing, mountaineering and ski touring. Ski touring is a form of skiing where you first ascend the mountain on skis before skiing down. The idea is to escape the crowds and find the more remote parts of the mountain and the freshest snow.

I like the physical challenge, making fresh tracks and going to quiet places in the mountains where you would otherwise not be able to go and where you have the most breathtaking views.
